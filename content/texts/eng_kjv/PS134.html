Psalm 134
1 Behold, bless ye the Lord, all ye servants of the Lord, which by night stand in the house of the Lord. 2 Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, and bless the Lord.1 3 The Lord that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion.
