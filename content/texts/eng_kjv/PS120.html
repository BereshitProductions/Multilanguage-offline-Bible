Psalm 120
1 In my distress I cried unto the Lord, and he heard me. 2 Deliver my soul, O Lord, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue. 3 What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue?12 4 Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper.3 5 Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar! 6 My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace. 7 I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war.4
1120:3What shall be given…: or, What shall the deceitful tongue give unto thee? or, what shall is profit thee?2120:3done: Heb. added3120:4Sharp…: or, It is as the sharp arrows of the mighty man, with coals of juniper4120:7for peace: or, a man of peace
