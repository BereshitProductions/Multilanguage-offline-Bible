Psalm 150
1 Praise ye the Lord. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power.1 2 Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness. 3 Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.2 4 Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.3 5 Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals. 6 Let every thing that hath breath praise the Lord. Praise ye the Lord.
