Psalm 70
Overview
70:1 David solicits God to the speedy destruction of the wicked, and preservation of the godly.

This Psalm is almost word for word the same as the five
Psa_40:13 of Psalm 40; and it is written as a part of the succeeding Psalm in about 27 manuscripts.
