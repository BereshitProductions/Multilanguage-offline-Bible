Acts 7:38 - lively Verse 2I am anThe life of Moses, the great prophet of Jehovah and lawgiver of the Jews, was exactly the same in length as the time Noah employed in preaching righteousness to the antediluvian world. These one hundred and twenty years were divided into three remarkable periods. Forty years he lived in Egypt, in the court of Pharaoh, acquiring all the learning and wisdom of the Egyptians - Acts 7:20, 7:23
forty years he sojourned in Midian, in a state of preparation for his great and important mission - 7:29, 7:30
and forty years he guided, led, and governed the Israelites under the express direction and authority of God: in all 120 years.
