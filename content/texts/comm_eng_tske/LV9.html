Verse 1the eighth dayNot on the eighth day of the month, but on the first day after their consecration, which occupied seven days, and before which they were deemed unfit to minister in holy things, being considered in a state of imperfection. All creatures, for the most part, were considered as in a state of uncleanness and imperfection, seven days, and perfected on the eighth. - See note on Leviticus 12:2, 12:3; 14:8-10; 15:13, 15:14; 22:27; Numbers 6:9, 6:10.
