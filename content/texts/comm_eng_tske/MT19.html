Overview
19:1 Christ heals the sick;
19:3 answers the Pharisees concerning divorcement;
19:10 shows when marriage is necessary;
19:13 receives little children;
19:16 instructs the young man how to attain eternal life;
19:20 and how to be perfect;
19:23 tells his disciples how hard it is for a rich man to enter into the kingdom of God;
19:27 and promises reward to those that forsake anything to follow him.
